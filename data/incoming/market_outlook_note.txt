INDIA PACKAGED DRINKING WATER — MARKET OUTLOOK (analyst note, FY26)

The Indian packaged drinking water category is estimated at a total addressable
market of roughly INR 403,000 mn in the current year, with the serviceable
addressable portion (organised, retail-reachable) at about INR 240,000 mn.

Category value is growing at a CAGR of approximately 13.2% (value) and 9.8%
(volume) over the FY23–FY26 window. Concentration remains moderate, with an
estimated HHI of 0.18; the top four players account for a little over half of
organised sales. The category is relatively young — the mean age of the leading
organised players is about 21 years — and growth shows mild deceleration versus
the FY19–FY22 period.
